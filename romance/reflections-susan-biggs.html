Kacie Buckingham desperately needs some time to put her pre-wedding jitters to rest. She spontaneously decides to take a few days and go white water rafting. What she expected was a few days of rest, relaxation and reflection. Instead she encounters a breathtakingly gorgeous tour guide and some haunting memories.

Mason Cash enjoys his job, but has to make changes to accommodate his family's needs. His last rafting expedition is more than he expects when he meets Kacie. She's beautiful and intriguing.

Instantly Mason and Kacie feel an attraction towards one another. Will this little adventure down the Colorado be the distraction they both need or the beginning of something neither expected?

Can these two find true love before they are devoured by the waves separating them? Will they be able to overcome the obstacles; her past and his family? Not to mention the ring on her hand and hundreds of miles they live apart.
