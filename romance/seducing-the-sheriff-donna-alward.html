When Cassidy Strong signs up for NotMy1stRodeo.com, she never expects it'll put her in the middle of a bank robbery--or that she'll be the key to breaking the case. It's safe to say that her first foray into online dating to jumpstart her love life after her divorce is an unqualified disaster...until a sexy sheriff arrives to save her.

Sheriff Joe Lawson is ready to do whatever it takes to solve the case, and that means protecting the lovely witness by hiding her away in his mountain cabin. Joe has a job to do, and acting on any attraction would go against everything he stands for. But sometimes doing what's right isn't as important as following your heart.
