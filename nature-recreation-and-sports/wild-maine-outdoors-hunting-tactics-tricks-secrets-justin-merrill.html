The "Northwoods Sporting Journal" "On The Prowl" columnist shares with you, "Wild Maine Outdoors - Hunting tactics, tricks, & secrets". This book was made up of five sections highlighting five topics written with wisdom gathered from the master teacher, the Wild Maine Outdoors. It's packed with hunting stories depicting game killing tactics that have been proven to work time and time again. Justin utilizes his Wildlife Biology Degree to enlighten you about the life/biology/physiology of the whitetail deer, black bear, bobcat, red fox, and the coyote. Justin's five years of teaching an Advanced Woods Survival course through the Maine Conservation School allows you to read about hunting tricks and tracking techniques that have fooled every one of these critters - as learned from Michael Douglas, who learned his skills from Tom Brown Jr. (The Tracker), who learned from the Apache Indian, Stalking Wolf (Grandpa). Justin shares a few of his secrets that are a must read.
