As a young boy, Frankie watched his older brother play lacrosse. Day after day, he dreamed of being a star lacrosse player, too. But, he did not believe that he could do it. Would its magic make Frankie into the lacrosse player he always wanted to be? The Magic Lacrosse Stick tells about a boys inspiring and heartwarming journey towards being not only a star athlete, but an individual with heart, soul, and confidence.
