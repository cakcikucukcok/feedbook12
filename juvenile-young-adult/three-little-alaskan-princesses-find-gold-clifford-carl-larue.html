Kaka Sa U, Goon A Teen, and little Sha What Gu are three little Alaskan princesses. The sisters live in the small village of Kake, which is located in the heart of southeastern Alaska.

The villagers who share their home work hard. They keep their village alive by hunting, fishing, and trapping. The children of the village help too. In the mornings they gather water, and in the evenings they gather wood. That leaves the rest of the day for building, playing, and dreaming. One day, Kaka Sa U has a dream of her own that will take her and her sisters on an extraordinary adventure.

The three princesses set out on an adventure to find gold to so that they can buy whatever they want. Join them princesses on their journey as they learn about what is really valuable in their lives. Together, they discover what it truly means to be rich.
