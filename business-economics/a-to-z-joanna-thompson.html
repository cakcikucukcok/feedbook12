Leaders who understand and are true to themselves are best equipped to lead others, and they attract followers. Deep personal reflection isn’t self-indulgent but a route to success and fulfilment, which is why much of the content in this practical dip-in guide is about personal development.

If you're looking for a clear and inspiring roadmap to professional success, look no further than international business coach Joanna Thompson's book debut A to Z: Your Navigator to Success. Joanna invites you to pick a letter of the alphabet, read, reflect and, above all, take action. With insightful chapters dedicated to hot topics such as authenticity, brand, resilience, strengths, vulnerability and quitting (yes, quitting!), this guide features a wealth of prompts and revelations that will refresh your approach and take you forward, whether you're an employee, entrepreneur or gig economy participant. Universal in its appeal and engaging in its honesty, Your Navigator to Success is an insightful compilation of 'shortcuts to the top' by someone who's been there, and a philosophical foundation for building a life of significance.
