The Blue Book, is an enrichment tool comprising a series of applied organizational excellence principles, designed to evoke critical thinking and encourage high performance at various organizational levels. Conceptualized to assist directors, management, technocrats and consultants in the attainment of sustained organizational excellence, Author, Jerry DaC Blenman provides specific steps, in the form of principles, to stimulate and optimize organizational excellence by thoughtful and rational discernment of organizational activities and behaviors. Simultaneously, the emphasis on immediacy of response by leaders is an important step he underscores towards eliminating deficiency gaps and achieving high performance within any organization. 

The Blue Book captures as a central theme, the actualization of organizational excellence as the basis for improved performance, optimization of opportunities for growth and the positioning of organizations to retain a long-term market presence.
