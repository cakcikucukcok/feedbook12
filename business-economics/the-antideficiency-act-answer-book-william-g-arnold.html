Avoid Violations of the Antideficiency Act! Antideficiency Act (ADA) violations within both the financial and audit communities are now at an all-time high. Violations often result from a lack of knowledge about what is and what is not permissible under the law. The Antideficiency Act Answer Book is an easy-to-understand question-and-answer tool that guides you through all the rules associated with the Antideficiency Act and helps you detect and report violations in a timely manner. Covers all aspects of the Antideficiency Act, from its history to common violations, penalties for violation, and reporting requirements Includes the tools you need to help avoid Antideficiency Act violations Plus! Includes an analysis of all the ADA reports collected by the Government Accountability Office, summarized by agency, appropriation, amount, and type of violation.
