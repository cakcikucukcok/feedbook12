Quotes that can speak to you can stay with you! Every day life looks like either something to love or a load to carry. When you want something to say to express how you feel, Stuff You Can Say Everyday contains one liners to do just that! Because of all of lifes challenges, this book is full of words that I internalized before I verbalized them. As I readily state, Use the crap from your past to fertilize your future. In so doing, may you find words that will challenge you to reach a new level in your lifeone liners that will make you laugh, love, and help you go to a deeper life spiritually. One sentence spoken to you in the right season could give your life more meaning than a hundred other reasons. Use Stuff You Can Say Everyday everywhere you go! Make your words unregrettable and worth retelling, so someone will find them unforgettable and worth repeating.
