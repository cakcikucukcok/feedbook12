View From the Second Row illustrates my own experiences as a pastors wife and illuminates my journey of faith in different areas of ministry. In a multicultural Caribbean dominated congregation, the book is about tolerance and faith as I stand upon the promises of Gods faithfulness and His love towards me. View From the Second Row places me in church, specifically in the pew where I am regularly seated. It places me in the attitude of worship as I recall the things that have occurred in my life leading up to twenty years as a Pastors wife. Having a story to tell prompted me to journal a fraction of my life to which each chapter in the book highlights my lifes familiarity.
