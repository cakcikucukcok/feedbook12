Ryuoh Yano is a Japanese mystery writer and a system engineer for a company. One day he began suffering from an inexplicable headache and fever. There are several reasons for hepatitis, any of which didn't seem to be applicable to Yano's case. However, behind his hepatitis hid the truth that he couldn't guess even though he was a mystery writer.
