Andy heads for the hills to clear his head, meets a mystic who gives him an eco-challenge, and then discovers he`s stirred up a hornets nest of opposition from his partner Liz. He also discovers that what should be a gentle eco-quest has provoked violent opposition from traditional land-owning interests. With each venture he embarks upon, the threats on his life grow in severity; he is in real danger. Undeterred, he continues, makes many fine discoveries in the wild landscapes of Scotland and moves ever closer to completing his mission. But will his relationship survive, and how will he make the very most of all his discoveries and revelations? Andy is a determined and very resourceful guy; the story of how he deals with all of the conflict around him will draw you into this gripping and romantic tale.
