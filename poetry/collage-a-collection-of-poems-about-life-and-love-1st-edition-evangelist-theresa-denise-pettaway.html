Merriam-Webster's Collegiate Dictionary defines a collage as "an assembly of diverse fragments". Evangelist Theresa Denise Pettaway (Evangelist Tee) has collected from her treasure trove of poems this ensemble of varying poetry styles and topics. This collection reflects a sampling of her life's experiences as expressed through the written word. Most of these poems were not written with any particular person in mind. However, if you should find a poem that you really like, you can borrow it as your own. Evangelist Tee's sincere hope and prayer is that these poems might somehow minister comfort and peace to your heart, spirit, and soul. ENJOY!!!
