Dont kill me on a Tuesday night

Allow the morning to come

The nights of Tuesday

There is a profane moon

The most insane of the madmen

Is starring at it

Verses got bored talking

About a sweet lovely moon

The signs of day show night

The signs of night show love

Dont kill me on a Tuesday night

Let me live

On a Tuesday night

With a profane moon

Alive
