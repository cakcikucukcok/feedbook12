Faith amid the Crosses is a collection of inspirational poems that incorporates faith, love, trust, endurance, and deliverance. It portrays a powerful woman character that infuses with faith in God and praises Him throughout her struggles. Its proponent is a vehicle for empowerment from evildoers to obliterate wickedness and able to overcome the troubles in life. It demonstrates that we are in fact co-creators with the Father and get better every day. If we accept Jesus in our lives, we have the ability to accomplish goals effectively and effortlessly. It becomes possible to accomplish any task or obstacle that crosses our pathways. It illustrates praises throughout her accomplishments to her Father from one task to another, acquiring blessings and strength to pursue her achievements. Her crosses were excruciating and arduous, but her trust in God supersedes her pains and difficulties and makes life manageable to survive. She portrays her trust in God; He was the central aspect throughout her crosses and causes her to succeed. In the interim, it also shows that the character has a close relationship with her Father and she frequently praises Him and acknowledges that He is amazingly merciful. Throughout her troubles she did not despair, or complaint, but carries her crosses with heads up.She trips, did not fall. She bends, did not break. In the end, she was victorious and becomes a better person spiritually and physically.
