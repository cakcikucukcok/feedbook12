If You Can't Be Good, Be Kind! By The Abbotts Everyone has temptations in their life and it is often difficult to overcome them or behave in a way that doesn't hurt those nearest and dearest to us. The Abbotts in this new book, give advice on how to be an individual and live your own life and values, but still be kind to others. They don't expect you to be perfect! With loving messages from angelic beings and good common sense, you can learn why incidents happen as they do and what happens, when you choose bad behaviour over good. Special exercises will help you to live a happier, more exciting life free of guilt and fear. A must-read for every Starseed and Lightworker who wants to know how and why! Subjects include - sex, love, finances, crime, drugs and addictions, mental and emotional pain and spirituality. A chance to be just a little more spiritual, in your own life!
