The most common cause of snoring is obesity. If you have a modern lifestyle and rely heavily on junk food, there is a strong possibility that you are overweight. The fatty tissue in the neck area can obstruct the airways and lead to snoring. Other common causes of snoring are alcohol consumption, smoking and medication. Some medications can cause muscles to relax and allow air to enter the body, causing snoring. The way or your sleeping position can also cause snoring. If your partner is lying on his back, they can usually snore a lot. there may be other serious causes, such as breathing problems or sinus problems. Don't forgive them. Snoring can be a major health problem, so it's good to consult a doctor and find out the real cause of the habit of snoring.
