Mankind no longer knows FAITH.  Evil is committed in the name of faith when FAITH is not present, a toxic form of belief has taken its place.  A split has formed within the psyche of mankind that separates the mind from the heart, the spirit from the soul and belief from FAITH.  It started with a wounded feeling function and has spread to the loss of soul and the separation of all mankind to the experience of FAITH.

We confuse the image with the reality, the picture and the person.  The true practice of values has been denigrated and reduced to fable.  Social media has augmented the disconnection and the discord.  Politics turns ugly.  Corporations run rampant destroying everything for monetary gain and short term vision.  Our connection to the sacred waits patiently for the awakening.  And still we slumber.

FAITHLESS is the story of Timothy.  Timothy has lived a life of sorrow.  Born with pneumonia, he died twice in his first month of life but was brought back to life with the miracle of modern medicine.  Timothy continued a lifetime out-maneuvering the odds.  Accidents, injuries, floods, lightning strikes, fire and the death of his daughter push Timothy to the edge of his sanity.  Yet, a power beyond imagination continues to thrive all around him.  Sorrow, pain, betrayal, abuse, shunning and despair shape and mold a unique man that hears the song of angels and sees the beauty of the earth unreconciled.

A message has come from beyond this physical realm and must be answered.  His daughter's spirit accompanied by her angel appear before Timothy with a message to deliver to all mankind.  The message must be delivered before it is too late.  And thus, FAITHLESS is born from the ashes of his grief, giving him purpose and direction for his indomitable spirit, the healing of his soul and a path to becoming whole once again.
