God promises that if we seek Him with all our hearts, we will find Him. Along lifes journey, He promises to keep our feet from slipping. When clouds come our way, He sends sun rays to burst through, unveiling His presence. In the midst of the clamoring world, He offers us peace.

Personal experiences described in Living in His Light offer encouragement for those looking for the reality of our Lords never-ending presence.
