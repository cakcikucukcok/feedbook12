We are excited, delighted with devotion, when we come to know about world's miraculous saints, yogis and Avatars. Our little mind and intellect are unable to understand their miracles and we left them unexplained. But due to explosion of scientific knowledge in the 20th century, we have now started to re-think and understand the miracles performed by them. Miracles are reality not myths and explainable also. Present Sciences are still in childish stage but ultimately it will explain the Miracles also. Our Universe is most miraculous and it is physical body of God. India has been great place for miraculous saints. It is a commendable research work of the author that he has collected huge information from various sources about so many great miraculous saints and yogis in this small book. Now the time has come for us not to ignore their miracles but to start further researches on these miracles and enlighten the world about the advance spiritual laws, which are still unknown to sciences.
