One of the most difficult challenges of becoming a new Christian is finding sound advice on how to start ones early walk in the basics of the Christian lifestyle. There are scores of books on the Bible, but the vast majority are for the mature Christians. Where are the books that lay out the basic for the newcomer? For the beginner in the faith, how often have you heard the advice from the church leaders and members, What you need to do next is get into a good church and start reading your Bible. Youve been there before and so have I. Oftentimes, this advice is a simple way of saying, Im not willing to take you under my wing at this time, but someone in your local church will give you good advice. In some cases, you may have met someone to help you with your journey, but they are only able to meet you one night during the week when youre working a second job. Your schedules just dont seem to fit. Or perhaps, you may have received a church elder who is willing to spend the time, but you dont seem to relate to that person due to a host of factorsage difference, culture, gender, or their peculiar mannerisms. If you fit into any one of these categories, or if you have had similar experiences of not finding a guide, then this book is for you.
